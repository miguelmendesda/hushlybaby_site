Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Hushly Baby helps caregivers create a calmer bedtime routine with soothing sounds, timer features, and safety guidance.

2. Data Collection

Hushly Baby does not require account creation and does not collect personal identity data directly from users.

3. Audio and Microphone

  • Audio playback works locally on your device.
  • If microphone-based features are enabled, microphone access is used only on-device for sound-level estimation.
  • No microphone recordings are uploaded, stored on external servers, or sold.

4. Offline Usage

Core sound playback features are available offline and do not require an internet connection.

5. Subscriptions and Purchases

Hushly Baby offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions and/or in-app purchases. Payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your full payment information.

6. Children’s Privacy

Hushly Baby is intended for parents/caregivers. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
